About

Desire to give wings to your startup and soar high above the giants? Upstart is just what you need. Upstart, a dream event curated specifically for budding startups. This event stands out from the conventional Start-up Competition and 7-minute pitching; we at Upstart, strive to give our participants more than just the prize, we are all about the experience. The participating start-up teams undergo multiple screening cycles comprising of brainstorming sessions at varying levels and one to one mentoring.

Ensuing the sweeping response of Upstart at IIT Kanpur, this year Upstart Bangalore edition has stemmed. This is a one-shot chance for startup geeks all over the nation to pitch their ideas before the best crew of venture capitalists, mentors and investors out there. The top 10 Startups will get direct entry to the Upstart Kanpur.

A Business plan competition like no other which offers you a 3-week mentoring session followed by intensive face to face mentoring with distinguished names from entrepreneurship world. If your budding startup is new in the business and seeking funding, incubation or mentorship aid, Upstart is the befitting platform for you. Here is your shot to give the push your startup requires, to make an impactful presence in the business. Selected startups will get the chance to interact with a group of leading entrepreneurs, who'll analyze every aspect of your business and look at your startup with the eye of a critique as well as a mentor. You have the chance, grab it!

Competition Structure

  1. One Step Registration:

    All participating startups have to register for Upstart Bangalore Only. The participants need to fill the concerned details and top startups will have a direct entry for the Upstart Kanpur Edition to be held at IIT Kanpur during E-Summit’19. After the Upstart Bangalore Finale, registration for Upstart-Kanpur are open to all.

  2. National Pitch - Bangalore Edition:

    The teams have to pitch their summary to an panel in under 7 minutes.It will be followed by 8 minute Question and Answer session by the panel. Pitch to the top Notch VCs and investors directly and receive valuable suggestions during the QnA session.

  3. Intensive Mentoring Sessions:

    The top 10 shortlisted startups from Upstart Bangalore will have the valuable chance of a Three - Week mentoring session at Upstart Kanpur with our illustrious mentors where they discuss their ideas and business plans to build a apropos legal, operational and structural framework. The major perks of the event will be intensive vis-à-vis mentoring and brainstorming sessions with a VC from the panel of Upstart, Kanpur and network boost possibility for startups.

  4. Final Pitch:

    The finale will be held at Upstart, IITK held during E-Summit’19. After the elevator pitch at IITK, Startups will have time till the next day to build upon their Plan and modify their Pitch based on the inputs they received. They will then participate in a 10-minute pitch (followed by Q&As) to present their improved Business Plans before the Upstart panel, a panel judges on 25th August. The start-ups will accordingly be given their final positions based on the Judging Criteria as elaborated in a later section.

Judging Criteria



  • Originality of Idea.
  • Scalability, in terms of the potential for growth and replication of the venture.
  • Well-conceived implementation plan and timetable.
  • Overall likelihood of achieving demonstrable results
  • Evidence of demand for the venture in the marketplace; market size and growth opportunity.
  • Demonstrated knowledge of target market
  • Competitive analysis , SWOT analysis
  • Marketing Plan addresses (wherever applicable): positioning, pricing, advertising, promotion, packaging, timetable.
  • Outline the development of systems to track, monitor and assess the performance milestones articulated in venture plan
  • Fundability: The potential for the venture to receive financial support from additional investors/donors(including opportunities for funding from foundations, social venture funds, angel investors and venture capitalists)
  • The rigor and feasibility of the venture's financial model and goals
  • Potential for follow-on funding to grow the venture through self-sufficiency or profitability.
  • Identify risk factors associated with the enterprise and external environment. Develop a plan for mitigating them.

  • Quantify venture leader's ability to develop people, promote change and achieve results
  • Improvement in the business plan from original proposal, post the mentoring session, will be taken into consideration.

Eligibility

As Upstart is a competition meant only for initial phase startups, we have put the following criteria for entry into the Final Round:

  • Your enterprise must have a prototype of its product or service and has conducted a significant number of pilots in the field, or must be already in operation. It must have conducted one pilot at least.
  • Enterprises which have been in operation for more than 3 years from the date of the incorporation will not be considered.
  • The Team should have atleast 1 and maximum 5 members.
  • The Startup should not have been VC funded. Startups financed through Bootstrapping or Seed Funding by Angel Investors are allowed to participate.
  • Startup entries must be the original work of participants i.e. team members competing must be the principal owners of the venture.
