Why upstart?

An event that caters to the budding startups of our country, Upstart - Our highlight - stands apart from the conventional Start-up Competition and 10-minute pitching; we strive to give our participants more than just the prize. The start-ups go through multiple rounds of screening, which are essentially brainstorming sessions at different levels and face to face mentoring. Finalists get to develop their ideas and hone their business plans with a panel of mentors.

  A Call From The VCs

Top 3 teams to skip the pile of decks and get a chance to directly get funded from top ventures in the industry. Unleash your potential and get funded. No more waiting !

  Incubate & Execute

Through UPSTART, the winner gets an incubation opportunity at NASSCOM 10000 Startups and SIDBI Innovation & Incubation Centre (SIIC) at IIT Kanpur, set up in collaboration with Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI).
