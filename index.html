Why upstart?

An event that caters to the budding startups of our country, Upstart - The Indian Business Model Competition - stands apart from the conventional Start-up Competition and 10-minute pitching. We strive to give our participants more than just the prize. The start-ups go through multiple rounds of screening, which are essentially brainstorming sessions at different levels, and face to face mentoring. Finalists get to develop their ideas and hone their business plans under the mentorship of a panel of mentors.
